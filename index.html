How to Use the Email Signature Generator

Creating a Signature

  1. Go to the Build Signature tab.
  2. Select your company (Indiana, Ohio, or National).
  3. Fill in your personal information.
  4. Toggle options for cell phone, address, and disclaimer as needed.
  5. Preview your signature in real-time.
  6. Click Save Signature to store it locally.

Managing Saved Signatures

  1. Go to the View Saved Signatures tab.
  2. You'll see a list of all your saved signatures.
  3. Click Edit to modify a signature.
  4. Click Delete to remove a signature.
  5. Click Copy to copy the HTML to your clipboard.

Adding Your Signature to Outlook

  1. Create or select a signature and click Copy to Clipboard.
  2. Open Outlook and go to File > Options > Mail > Signatures.
  3. Click New to create a new signature and give it a name.
  4. Position your cursor in the editing area below.
  5. Paste your copied signature using Ctrl+V. You should see the fully formatted signature appear.
  6. Click Save then OK to finish.

Important: When copying from this tool, it copies the rendered HTML content, not the raw HTML code. This allows you to paste directly into Outlook's signature editor while maintaining all formatting.
